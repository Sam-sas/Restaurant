Restaurant Description

We love to eat. With friends, family, and the occasional co-worker after a long day its good to kick back and enjoy all-american flavours we've known since kids. A restaurant full of nostalgia, with experienced chefs and the goal to create a connection between eachother. Queen's Eatery is more than a local watering hole. We understand environmental impact, and make sure we only source from sustainable, local farmers.
